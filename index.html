Welcome to Music Catcher!

To play the game, you must click the correct notes and play the song!

Be careful, if you click the wrong notes or you let the notes hit the floor you will loose points!

Watch out for the chords, you will need to do a swipe between the keys O -> K -> M -> K -> O
Simple pressing them won't work!

Too Easy?
Hold down the spacebar for an extra sped up challenge!
(and a chance to earn extra points!)
